## History of phising

- from early days of internet (beginning in mid 1990) with attacks on America online (AOL) users
- later phising attacks expanded to email, web, more recently social media, mobile apps, and SMS

### Timeline of Evolution

- 1990s: early phising attack on AOL using fake website, instant messages to obtain login credentials
- 2000s: became more widespread with the use of email. fake emails to impersonate banks and other services
- 2010s: sphere phising(advanced) targest individual or orgs with personal messages
- present: sms, vishing(Voice phising), social media phising, adapting to new communicatinon and  tech

## Evolution of Techniques

- pharming: Attackers redirect legit website to fraud sites to capture user information.
- spear phising: A targeted form of phising aimed at specific individual annd org
- clone phising: attackers duplicate legit emails, ans resend them with malicious links or attachments
